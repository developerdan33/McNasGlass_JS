About

Brenton McNamara

McNa's Glass is owned and locally operated by Brenton McNamara. Brenton started his glazing career in New Zealand before making his way over the ditch, falling for an Aussie girl with whom he married and started a family with two children. With his love for the industry and drive to provide for his family, McNa's Glass came to fruition.

Brenton is not just known for his high-quality service and high standards but for his high values. His approach, attitude, and how he treats his clients make him second to none.
