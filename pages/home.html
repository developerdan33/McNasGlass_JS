About

Family Owned

McNa's Glass is a family-owned and operated company based in Eastern Heights, servicing the city of Ipswich and the greater region. We provide a genuine, quality service with a wealth of knowledge and experience within the glazing industry.

24 Hour Service

Broken glass can be a severe health hazard for you, your family or your business. Our professional glaziers are qualified and are on call 24/7 for emergency repair service. You can rest assured we will clean up the site and dispose of glass, to eliminate further damage or harm.

Customer Service

We pride ourselves on being a reliable, friendly, affordable service that has our customers' best interests at heart. Our staff will communicate honestly with you no matter the problem to achieve the best and safest solution.
